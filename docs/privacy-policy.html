Zenly — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 7 July 2026

Zenly (“we”, “the app”) is a focus and digital-wellbeing app. We designed it to be private by default. This policy explains what happens to your information.

1. We do not collect your data

Zenly has no user accounts and no servers of our own. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information. There is no analytics, advertising, or tracking in the app.

2. Data stored on your device

Your focus sessions, schedules, profiles, goals, challenges, and settings are stored locally on your device (and, if you enable it in a future update, synced privately through your own iCloud account via Apple CloudKit). This data never leaves your device except through your own iCloud, which is controlled by Apple and your Apple ID.

3. Screen Time (Family Controls)

To block distracting apps during focus sessions, Zenly uses Apple’s Screen Time framework (Family Controls, DeviceActivity, and ManagedSettings). When you select apps or websites to block, Apple provides Zenly with opaque tokens — the app never receives the real names of your apps, your browsing, or your usage in human-readable form. This information stays on your device and is managed by iOS. Zenly cannot see it and does not upload it.

4. Permissions we request (all used on-device)

  • Screen Time / Family Controls — to shield the apps you choose during focus.
  • Notifications — focus/break reminders and schedule start alerts.
  • Calendar — to suggest and auto-start focus during your free time.
  • Reminders — to sync your focus tasks, if you use that feature.
  • Apple Music — to control focus music playback from your library.

You can change or revoke any of these in the iOS Settings app at any time.

5. Third-party services

Music control is optional. If you choose to connect Spotify, your interaction with Spotify is governed by Spotify’s own privacy policy (spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy). If you do not connect Spotify, no data is shared with it. Apple Music is controlled locally through iOS.

6. Children

Zenly is a self-directed tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

7. Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes it. If iCloud sync is enabled in the future, you can remove the data from your iCloud storage.

8. Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above will change accordingly.
